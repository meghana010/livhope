Frequently Asked Questions (FAQS)

Volunteering:

Can I have the choices about the types of work Iam interested in?

Ofcourse , the answer is yes . We believe that ' Outcomes are better when you do something you love ' .

Does the organisation accept everyone who fills up the volunteer form ?

No , it has some process to filter the applicants .

How does the Organisation, filter out the volunteer applicants ?

Once we receive the forms , we send google forms to your mobile number or mail . Based on the responses , we finalise the volunteers.But in some emergency cases , we might accept all applicants .

Who is eligible to become a volunteer ?

Anyone with age greater than 16 and lesser than 56 , with a zeal to serve the people .

How do you pay ?

This is a non-profitable organisation . Therefore , we don't pay anything apart from provision of awards , rewards , certificates based on the services you provide to the people and organisation .

Donation:

How much of my donation is going to people in need ?

We ensure that 100% of your donation will go towards the needy . Please kindly note that 

                                          
                            We're not in it for income , 
                            We're in it for outcome .

Can I donate anything other than cash (like clothes,food) ?

Yes, you can .

How do I approach , when I want to donate something in offline mode ?

Please fill the donation form , fill the comments and the contact details without fail . Our team will contact you with the help of details provided and will let you know the way .

How will my donation be used ?

Your donation will be used to serve the needy people or for developing disaster-hit areas and remote areas .
